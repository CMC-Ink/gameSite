The Good

Video games can be a good as they entertain many people throughout the world by interacting with the game while watching. They also can be a means to relax or calm down when you are stressed out from problems that your facing.

Friendships

• Players can become friends outside of the game.

• Video game players can introduce people to each other.

• Relationships can easily be created while playing.

• In 2017 48% of people were playing socially online.

• In 2017 players played 6.5 hours per week playing online with others.

Anger Management

• Video games can help people vent out their anger.

• People become calmer after playing video games.

• Video games can be an alternative when it comes to getting anger out.

Leisure Time

• Video games can be excellent to play when you need to waste time.

• When people are tired of playing games they can easily do another activity.

• After playing video games players thoughts can be focused on other tasks.

• In 2015 on an average day, people spent up to 25 minutes playing video games to relax during their leisure time.

Role-Playing

• These games can allow players to experience things that they could never accomplish in real life.

• Players can live out a dream or fantasy that they have.

• This genre is one of the most popular in the industry.

Journeys & Adventures

• Players can set out on amazing journeys while playing.

• Games can be visually interest to players while adventuring.

• Players can understand a story as they progress within the game.

Relaxation

• After a hard days work players can relax while playing video games.

• When people are stressed out from overworking they can take their minds off of work and play a game to calm down.

• Playing video games can both relax and calm people down.

The Bad

However, video games do come with there share of problems too. If your not careful they can completely control your life or worse make it even more problematic. They can cause problems that can cause you to have situations in life that can affect you badly.

Anti-Social

• Some players never interact with others in the outside world.

• Players will play video games than meeting friends and doing other activities.

• Some players will act unsociable when meeting others outside of the game

• In 2016 the majority percentage of people playing solo gameplay is 67%.

Aggressive Behavior

• In many cases players will often get too deep while playing a game and can get aggressive when they do bad.

• They either will lash out at people or objects to get their anger out.

• When players really get angry they can resort to breaking things around them.

Loosing Track of Time

• Many players can easily loose track of time while playing video games.

• Players can spend hours on video games and can easily forget anything as they play.

• This can cause them to be deprived of sleep, eating, and doing other activities.

Delusional Fantasy

• These games can allow players to experience things that they could never accomplish in real life.

• Players can live out a dream or fantasy that they have.

• This genre is one of the most popular in the industry.

Isolation

• Players often isolate themselves in their rooms and play video games.

• They will spend hours of their time inside playing games instead of going outside.

• This can cause people to develop anti-social and unhealthy problems.

• The average amount of time spent playing video games in the United States is 18 hours per week, in a year that would be 6570 hours.

Procrastination

• Players will often tend to ignore others while playing.

• They’ll also procrastinate with doing work and chores.

• Problems can develop with health, finances, family, emotions, and communication.

What's the median?

After seeing both the good and bad sides to video games, has this changed your opinion on them? What do you think of them now? Well, if your worried don't because it's true that video games have problems but they also have some good qualities to them. The thing is to learn and understand the median between the two. Understand how to play video games the right way.

Time Yourself

• Keep an eye on the time to see how long you’ve been playing.

• The average amount of time to spend playing is one to two hours.

• More than five hours playing video games is considered to be too much.

Balance your Activities

• The best time to play is when there’s nothing to do.

• Make sure you know how long it will take to play.

• Stop when your done or have something to do.

Just Walk Away

• If you get frustrated while playing, take a 15-20 minutes break.

• When your stuck in the game, play an easier mode till your ready.

• If you cannot take it anymore then just stop and come back when your ready.

It’s Just a Game

• Remember it’s just a game, it’s not a life threatening situation.

• When you get mad just calm down and relax, take a deep breath to calm down.

• Never take a game too seriously, just have fun playing your game.

Having Fun with Friends

• If you feel lonely playing by yourself, then get your friends to play with you.

• Find people to play with you like friends and family that you know.

• Don’t spend all your time on video games with your friends, do other activities too.
